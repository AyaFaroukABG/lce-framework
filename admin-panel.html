Commercial · Pricing
Pricing
All pricing parameters are configurable per service type, vehicle category, pickup-dropoff pair, and booking channel. Two formula models supported. Toggle between upfront and dynamic pricing per area.
Pricing formula — configurable parameters
Option 1 — Distance based (closest to current)
=Base Priceconfigurable
+(Km × Price/km)configurable
+(Trip time × Price/min)configurable
×Surge factorconfigurable
+Waiting Time feeconfigurable
+Service FeeTBD
Option 2 — Price bracket
=Price Bracket (by km range)configurable
+(Trip time × Price/min)configurable
×Surge factorconfigurable
+Waiting Time feeconfigurable
+Service FeeTBD
Bracket example:
50–99 km = 30 EGP/km
100–149 km = 25 EGP/km
Base = 300 EGP
Trip 100 km = 300 + 25×100 = 2,800 EGP
