90-minute lock horizon protects near-departure trips. Changes only applied if saving exceeds threshold. Churn penalty prevents marginal reassignments.

trip.completed → capacity freed
Re-optimisation trigger 1 (new capacity) — ON HOLD bookings evaluated for promotion
ON HOLD riders get 15-minute re-confirm window after promotion. SAP reconciliation and rating do not interact with the engine.

Trip type known at booking
